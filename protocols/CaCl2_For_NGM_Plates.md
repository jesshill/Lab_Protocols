To a 1 L flask add:
- 110.98 g CaCl<sub>2</sub>
- Bring up to 1 L with ddH<sub>2</sub>0   


To a 500 mL flask add:
- 55.5g CaCl<sub>2</sub>
- Bring up to 500 mL with ddH20   

Filter sterilize, do not autoclave.
